About VacParts Direct

VacParts Direct is a Shenzhen-based industrial pressure switch supplier, located in the heart of China's manufacturing and export hub. Founded by a technical professional with over a decade of experience in vacuum systems, train maintenance, and PCB/PCBA design, we bring engineering-level product knowledge to every client interaction.

We partner directly with LEFOO and other certified manufacturers to provide high-quality pressure switches at factory-direct pricing. Our specialties include vacuum switches, differential pressure switches, refrigeration switches, and air compressor pressure controls — all UL and CE certified. With DDP door-to-door delivery to UAE (8-12 working days), flexible payment terms (50% deposit, 50% before shipment), and full OEM/private label capabilities (MOQ 1,000 pcs), we help distributors and equipment manufacturers build their own branded product lines without the overhead of factory ownership.
