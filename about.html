Who We Are

Shenzhen Xinyucheng Trading Co., Ltd., operating under the brand VacParts Direct, is a specialized exporter of industrial vacuum and pneumatic components.

Founded in Shenzhen — the manufacturing heart of China — we bridge the gap between world-class Chinese factories and industrial clients worldwide. Our core focus is the Middle East market, particularly the UAE and Dubai.

As an authorized LEFOO distributor, we offer the complete range of LEFOO pressure switches, vacuum regulators, and related pneumatic components with guaranteed authenticity and factory-direct pricing.
